## The Gap

While national Earth observation missions such as ISRO's Cartosat-2 series have provided valuable geospatial data, they are optimized for broad national scale monitoring rather than high-frequency hyper-local observation. Their operational constraints include:

- Limited revisit frequency over specific regions
- Restricted tasking flexibility
- Data dissemination pipelines that do not adequately support near-real-time decision-making at district or panchayat levels.

Currently, there is no dedicated, low-cost, high-resolution Earth observation platform tailored specifically to:

- Rapid flood extent and progression mapping at sub-meter scale
- Fine-scale urban growth and wetland encroachment monitoring
- Agriculture decision support thriugh timely vegetation condition assessment
